Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

At Latter Study, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect non-personal usage data such as device type, app version, and how often you use certain features. We do not collect or store personal data unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., for support).

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used to improve app performance, suggest features, and provide a smoother experience. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Security

All data is stored securely using modern cloud infrastructure. Sensitive content (such as lesson drafts or notes) is only accessible to you unless explicitly shared.

4. Children’s Privacy

Latter Study is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@latterstudy.com.
